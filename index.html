airtickets® is an online travel agency specialised in providing quality services to help you find and purchase air tickets, hotel accommodation, car rental, transfer, as well as tours & activities worldwide. Our goal is to ensure excellent customer experience and offer innovative tools and services to simplify the process of finding, planning and purchasing the right product at the lowest price. Our team consists of experienced agents with excellent knowledge of the travel market, as well as the special needs of the modern traveller. airtickets® operates under the management of Airtickets Travel Services S.A. . Having accumulated more than 10 years of experience and know-how in providing travel services, it has a leading role in the development of online travel in South-eastern Europe. With headquarters in London and local presence in Greece, Cyprus, Russia, Poland and Turkey, Airtickets Travel Services S.A. guarantees top quality travel services, excellent customer service and the best prices the market has to offer.
